Admission Process

Follow these four simple steps to secure your child's place at Pison.

1. Application

Fill out the online application form or visit our school office to collect a physical form.

2. Assessment

Prospective students will undergo a friendly placement interview and entry assessment.

3. Review

Our admissions committee reviews the assessment and application within 3-5 business days.

4. Enrollment

Once accepted, complete the registration fee payment to secure your child's seat.
