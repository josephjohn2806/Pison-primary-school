About Our School

Building a strong foundation for lifelong learning

Who We Are

Pison Pre & Primary School is a nurturing learning community dedicated to providing quality and affordable education to all deserving children without discrimination. We focus on academic excellence, character development, and holistic growth to prepare pupils for a successful future.

Our Vision, Mission & Motto

Our Vision

To ensure quality and affordable education is provided to all deserving children without discrimination of any kind, enabling every child to become the best and a responsible citizen of the country.

Our Mission

Through modern education pedagogy, to offer holistic education focusing on the mental, spiritual, and social faculties of a child.
